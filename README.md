# E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes over 541,000 retail transactions from an international e-commerce retailer between December 2010 and December 2011.

The goal was to identify key revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, geographic trends, and seasonal sales dynamics.

## Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Findings

### Revenue Concentration

- The United Kingdom generated 83.9% of total revenue.

### International Markets

- The Netherlands generated the highest revenue per transaction (£120).

### Seasonality

- Revenue exhibited a strong Q4 spike, suggesting significant holiday demand.

### Customer Concentration

- The top 10% of customers generated 60% of customer revenue.

### Product Performance

- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER was the highest revenue-generating product.
- JUMBO BAG RED RETROSPOT ranked highly in both revenue and sales volume.

## Recommendations

- Explore expansion opportunities in high-value international markets.
- Prioritize retention of high-value customers.
- Prepare inventory and staffing for Q4 demand spikes.
