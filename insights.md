# Key Insights

## Geographic Analysis

The UK generated 83.9% of total revenue, indicating a highly concentrated domestic market.

## Customer Analysis

Revenue was concentrated among high-value customers, with the top 10% contributing 60% of customer revenue.

## Seasonality

Revenue increased sharply during Q4, suggesting strong holiday-driven demand.

## Products

REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER generated the highest revenue among merchandise products.

JUMBO BAG RED RETROSPOT ranked highly in both revenue and volume, making it a particularly valuable product.
